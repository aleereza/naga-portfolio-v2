I had the privilege of documenting the heartwarming moments at the ["Go and Live"](https://www.amazon.com/Go-Live-Supriya-Singh/dp/B0CJHPBNVW/ "Go and Live") book giveaway event as the official photographer. This project beautifully captures the essence of author interactions, book signings, and candid moments that unfolded throughout the event.
