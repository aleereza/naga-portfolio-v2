In commemoration of [Camp Artaban's](https://www.campartaban.com/artaban-100 "Camp Artaban's") remarkable century-long journey from 1923 to 2023, I was commissioned to create captivating aerial photographs and videos that would serve as a visual tribute to their rich history and vibrant present. These stunning visuals were crafted to grace their social media platforms, adding an extra layer of grandeur to their centennial festivities.
